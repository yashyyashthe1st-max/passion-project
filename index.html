About this thesis

The core idea here is simple: web design isn't just decoration. It teaches creative thinking, supports business goals by generating traffic and sales, and—when done right—creates an experience users remember. Below are three pillars that connect the thesis to actionable practice.

Express creativity

Design exercises that unlock new visual languages: moodboards, rapid prototyping, and constraint-driven challenges.

Generate traffic & sales

SEO-first structure, persuasive copy, and conversion-focused layouts increase reach and revenue.

Promote unforgettable experiences

Microcopy, motion, and thoughtful interactions make products trustworthy and memorable.
